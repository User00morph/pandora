## WHAT THIS DOMAIN IS

Kemetic research is dedicated to the esoteric knowledge systems that originated in or were transmitted through ancient Egypt (Kemet). This includes the mystery school teachings, cosmological frameworks, soul science, and initiatic wisdom that preceded and seeded Western philosophy, religion, and science — most of which was appropriated without attribution.

This domain deconstructs those systems in their original form and reconstructs them as sovereign operating principles for Pandora OS.
