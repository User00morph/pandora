**3. Wyoming LLC + DAO LLC = sovereign business architecture for DeFi-native operations.**
Wyoming recognized DAOs as LLCs in 2021 (Wyoming DAO LLC Act). Key advantages:
- DAO LLC has legal personhood — can hold assets, sign contracts, receive payments
- Members are token holders — governance is on-chain
- Pass-through taxation (like standard LLC) — no corporate tax layer
- No public disclosure of member identity required in Wyoming
- Integrates with DeFi rails natively — the DAO treasury is a smart contract wallet
