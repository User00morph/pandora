### EVIDENCE ENTRY 3: MELANIN AS SUPERCONDUCTOR/SEMICONDUCTOR

**SOURCE:** Young Pharaoh
**TIER:** 3
**CLAIM:** Melanin is a semiconductor and superconductor of light and sound.
**FACT CHECK:**
- **Semiconductor:** Partially supported. McGinness et al. (1974), Science — demonstrated that melanin exhibits semiconductor-like switching behavior (threshold switching from resistive to conductive state). This is a Tier 1 publication in Science. However, melanin is an *amorphous* semiconductor, not a crystalline one like silicon. Its conductivity is limited.
- **Superconductor:** Not supported. Superconductivity (zero electrical resistance at low temperatures) has not been demonstrated in melanin. This is a misuse of the physics term.
- **Sound absorption:** Supported. Melanin does absorb acoustic energy.

**WHAT HOLDS:** Melanin IS a semiconductor (demonstrated in Science, 1974). It absorbs sound. It is a genuine multifunctional biomolecule.
**WHAT BREAKS:** Calling it a "superconductor" is incorrect by physics definition. This overclaim should be corrected to "semiconductor with unique switching properties."
**CONTRADICTION FLAG:** The semiconductor claim is scientifically supported. The superconductor claim is not. **Logging at full strength because overclaiming weakens the legitimate science.**

### EVIDENCE ENTRY 4: BLACK BODY RADIATION & MELANATED BODIES

**SOURCE:** Young Pharaoh
**TIER:** 3
**CLAIM:** The universe's background radiation is that of a "black body" (2.725 K), and melanated people are embodiments of this cosmic radiation principle.
**FACT CHECK:**
- **CMB as black body:** Established (Tier 1). The cosmic microwave background has the most perfect black body spectrum ever measured (COBE/FIRAS, 1990).
- **"Black body" in physics:** An idealized object that absorbs ALL incident electromagnetic radiation and re-emits it as thermal radiation. The term describes absorption/emission behavior, not color or race.
- **Melanin as approximation of black body:** Partially valid. Melanin's broadband absorption across the EM spectrum IS functionally similar to a black body's absorption profile. No biological material is a perfect black body, but melanin is among the closest approximations in biology.

**WHAT HOLDS:** The parallel between melanin's broadband absorption and black body behavior is *scientifically meaningful*, not merely metaphorical. Melanin is genuinely one of the most efficient broadband absorbers in biology.
**WHAT STRETCHES:** Conflating cosmological black body radiation with human melanin biology ignores scale, context, and mechanism differences. The CMB is thermal radiation from the early universe at 2.725 K. Human melanin operates at body temperature absorbing ambient environmental radiation. They share an absorption *principle* but not a *mechanism*.
**SOVEREIGN NOTE:** The parallel is powerful as a framework for understanding melanin's significance. It should be presented as "melanin exhibits black-body-like absorption properties" rather than "melanated people are the cosmos."
