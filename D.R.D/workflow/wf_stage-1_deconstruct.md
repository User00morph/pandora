# WF_DRD — STAGE 1: DECONSTRUCTION
**Break the topic into its component questions before researching anything.**

---

## WHAT THIS STAGE IS

No topic is a single question. This stage maps what actually needs to be answered before any research begins. A poorly mapped topic produces unfocused research. A precisely mapped topic produces precise findings.

---

## ACTION

**1. ASSIGN TO A RESEARCH DOMAIN**
```
1. Human History & Origins
2. Suppressed & Alternative Science
3. Pre-Western Knowledge Systems
4. The Divine Feminine
5. Consciousness Research
6. Systemic Analysis
```
If it crosses domains — assign primary domain, note secondaries.

**2. MAP THE SUB-QUESTIONS**
Take the stated topic and write out every sub-question it contains. Do not answer yet — only map.

Example:
> Topic: "Does ashwagandha reduce cortisol?"
> Sub-questions:
> - What is cortisol and how is it measured?
> - What does ashwagandha actually contain?
> - What studies exist on this claim?
> - What populations were studied?
> - What dosages and forms were used?
> - What were the effect sizes?
> - Are there contradicting studies?
> - What are the confounding factors?

**3. STATE THE EXISTING POSITION** *(if one exists)*
What is currently believed about this topic inside the OS? Write it plainly. This is not to defend it — it is to make the bias visible so research can account for it.

---

## OUTPUT

A numbered list of sub-questions with domain assignment. This list governs all subsequent stages.

---

## GATE

> Is the topic stated clearly enough that sub-questions can be written?
> If not — clarify the topic first. Do not begin Stage 2 on a vague question.
