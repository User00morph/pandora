# WF_DRD — STAGE 5: RECONSTRUCTION
**Build the sovereign position from honest evaluation of everything found.**

---

## WHAT THIS STAGE IS

This is where D.R.D produces its actual output — not just what the sources say, but what the sovereign mind concludes after honest evaluation of all of it. The distillations from Stage 4 are the building blocks.

**Load: `shared/skills/skill_framework-synthesis.md`** for complex multi-domain reconstructions.

---

## ACTION

The reconstruction answers six questions:

**1. WHAT IS NOW KNOWN WITH HIGH CONFIDENCE?**
Findings supported by Tier 1–2 consensus. State these directly.

**2. WHAT IS PROBABLE BUT NOT ESTABLISHED?**
Findings supported by strong Tier 3 evidence or Tier 1–2 evidence with meaningful limitations. State these with explicit probability language.

**3. WHAT REMAINS GENUINELY UNCERTAIN?**
Contradictions that could not be resolved. Findings where only Tier 4–5 evidence exists. State the uncertainty plainly — do not paper over it.

**4. WHAT WAS BELIEVED BEFORE THAT REQUIRES REVISION?**
If the research found something that contradicts an existing OS position — name it directly. This is the most sovereign act of the entire workflow.

**5. WHAT DOES THIS MEAN FOR THE REQUESTING DEPARTMENT?**
Translate findings into the specific context of whoever requested the research. What changes? What does this enable? What should be reconsidered?

**6. WHAT FOLLOW-UP RESEARCH WOULD STRENGTHEN WEAK FINDINGS?**
Identify the specific questions that remain open and what sources would resolve them. Add these to `DRD_INDEX.md` under QUEUED RESEARCH.

---

## CRITICAL RULE

> The reconstruction must be honest about uncertainty.
> "The evidence suggests X, though Y remains contested" is a stronger sovereign position than false certainty.
> False certainty fails when tested. Honest uncertainty can be built on.

---

## OUTPUT STRUCTURE

```
## FINDINGS
[High confidence findings — stated directly]

## CONFIDENCE LEVELS
[Each finding: ESTABLISHED / PROBABLE / POSSIBLE / CONTESTED / NEEDS EVIDENCE]

## IMPLICATIONS
[What this means for the requesting department and the OS]

## OPEN QUESTIONS
[What follow-up research would address, with domain and priority]
```

## FILING

Save as: `reconstructions/[domain]/drd_reconstruct_[topic]_reconstructed.md`
Update: `DRD_INDEX.md` — move stage to `reconstructed`, assign confidence level
