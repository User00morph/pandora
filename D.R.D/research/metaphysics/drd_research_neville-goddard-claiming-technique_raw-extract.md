## Key Concepts Extracted

**The Goddard Correction (vs. conventional prayer):**
- Conventional: asking + wanting + lack consciousness = reinforces poverty/absence
- Goddard: claiming + having consciousness + absorbed identity = manifest from inside out
- Reality mirrors inner state, not outer words/requests

**Core Technique Steps:**
1. Define desire specifically
2. Enter imagination (shut out external world completely)
3. Become the desire — not observe it from outside, fully inhabit it from inside
4. Stay until it feels like identity, not practice
5. Exit as the new person — maintain that identity in daily life

**Key Distinctions:**
- Asking vs. Claiming: you claim what's already yours; you ask for what you think isn't
- Seeing vs. Being: visualization from outside reinforces separation; inhabiting from inside creates merger
- Touching vs. Absorbing: brief contact vs. full identity absorption

**The Two-Person Test:**
- Person 1 (at the gate) = asking, hoping, waiting = "this isn't mine yet"
- Person 2 (living in the house) = claiming, owning, being = "this is mine now"
- This is the exact energy distinction — identical inner posture is required
