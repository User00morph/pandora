## Key Concepts Extracted

**Why DeFi Matters for Sovereignty:**
- No central authority = no account freezing, no discrimination, no reporting
- $10M transfers for $5 fee vs. banking's $10K threshold reporting requirement
- "The code is the law" = immutable rules no government can override
- Censorship-resistant banking for ANY business type including legal gray areas

**The 5 Pillars:**
1. **Stablecoins** — USDC/DAI/Tether: bridge between crypto and USD value without bank intermediary
2. **Lending/Borrowing** — Compound/Aave: earn interest or borrow against holdings without selling; over-collateralized; fully automated via smart contract
3. **DEXes** — Uniswap/PancakeSwap: swap any token for any token at <0.5% fee; immutable code; no regulatory gatekeeping
4. **Insurance** — Oracle-triggered smart contracts; no insurance company needed; premium pooling automated
5. **Margin Trading** — Leveraged positions without identity verification; automated liquidation; open globally

**Flash Loans (advanced):**
- Borrow millions with zero collateral for a 10-second transaction window
- Arbitrage mechanism: borrow → buy low on exchange A → sell high on exchange B → repay → keep spread
- Impossible in traditional finance — DeFi-exclusive instrument

**For Morph's Sovereign Business Architecture:**
- Trust holds crypto assets = no capital gains on trust-held assets
- DEX trading = no KYC, no reporting to IRS (unlike Coinbase/Gemini)
- Stablecoins as operating currency = stay in crypto ecosystem without touching banking system
- Lending/borrowing against holdings = liquidity without taxable sale event
- "The code is the law" = exit from legal person commercial jurisdiction
