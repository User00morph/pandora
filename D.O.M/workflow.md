# WF_DOM_PRACTICE_DEVELOPMENT
**D.O.M — Department of Magik**
**Workflow: From Impulse to Integrated Practice**

---

## WHAT THIS WORKFLOW IS

The process for developing, testing, and integrating new magical practices, rituals, or working methods into Morph's sovereign system. Also used when revisiting existing practices that need refinement or retirement.

---

## TRIGGER

- An intuitive impulse toward a new practice or working
- A specific intention that needs a ritual or practice designed for it
- A lunar, planetary, or seasonal timing that calls for structured work
- An existing practice that has stopped producing results
- A new concept from study (chaos magik, Kemetic practice, energy science) that needs practical testing

---

## THE WORKFLOW — STEP BY STEP

### STAGE 1 — IMPULSE CAPTURE (Nigredo)
**Receive the raw creative-spiritual impulse.**

```
ACTION:
Document what is calling for attention:
- What intention or question is driving this?
- What inspired it? (internal impulse, study, timing, life event)
- What does the desired outcome feel like or look like?
Do not design yet — just receive.

OUTPUT: dom_impulse_[name]_raw.md
```

---

### STAGE 2 — RESEARCH AND DESIGN (Albedo)
**Study the relevant traditions and design the practice.**

```
ACTION:
1. TRADITION RESEARCH: What do relevant frameworks say about
   this type of working? (Chaos magik, Kemetic, astrological,
   energy science, etc.) Note: D.O.M is governed by direct
   experience, not source tiers — but study informs design.

2. DESIGN: Use the ritual design process to build the practice.

SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_ritual-design.md
OUTPUT: dom_design_[name]_v1.md
```

---

### STAGE 3 — PRACTICE (Citrinitas)
**Perform the practice and observe results.**

```
ACTION:
- Execute the designed ritual or practice
- Observe with full attention — what happens during?
- Document immediately after:
  - Internal state before, during, after
  - Any synchronicities or external shifts observed
  - What felt potent and what felt empty
  - What would you change next time?

OUTPUT: dom_log_[name]_YYYY-MM-DD.md
```

---

### STAGE 4 — EVALUATION (Rubedo)
**Assess whether this practice earns a place in the system.**

```
ACTION:
After sufficient practice (minimum 3 repetitions for repeating
practices, or one full cycle for seasonal work):

1. RESULTS: Did it produce the intended outcome?
   Fully / partially / not at all?

2. INTERNAL QUALITY: Did the practice feel coherent
   and alive, or mechanical and dead?

3. REFINEMENT: What needs to change?
   Components to add, remove, or modify?

4. DECISION:
   INTEGRATE — add to the active practice system
   REFINE — redesign and re-test
   RETIRE — this practice does not serve, release it

OUTPUT: dom_eval_[name]_assessment.md
```

---

### STAGE 5 — INTEGRATION OR RETIREMENT
**File the practice in its final state.**

```
If INTEGRATE:
- Document the finalized practice: dom_practice_[name]_active.md
- Add to D.O.M active practices register
- Note frequency and timing

If RETIRE:
- File with the assessment: dom_practice_[name]_retired.md
- Document why — this is data for future practice design
```
