## LAYER 1 — THE SEQUENCING LAW

Before any casting, the practitioner's system must be sequenced correctly. Casting specific spells into a blocked or missequenced field produces minimal results — not because magic doesn't work, but because internal resistance counteracts incoming force.

**Mandatory sequence:**
```
1. Stabilize nervous system → freeze disruptive elements (bill jar, freeze spell)
2. Set priority hierarchy → needs over wants (written list)
3. Open roads → clear path of blockages (road opener candle work)
4. Bust blockages → break internal resistance (daily blockbuster work)
5. Cast specific work
```

**Resistance diagnostic:**
If magic is barely working, the issue is almost always internal safety/worthiness architecture, not technique. Lack of safety creates bidirectional barriers — blocking bad AND good equally. The fix is not more spells. It is daily self-directed resistance work until the body feels it.

**Affirmation sequencing protocol (from resistance to specific):**
- Phase 1 (general): "I am safe. I am worthy. I deserve it." — hold until it lands in body
- Phase 2 (directional): "I am safe from harm unknown. I am worthy of luxury. I deserve all abundance." — hold until easy
- Phase 3 (specific): name exact desires — cast targeted work from here

**Tool: Freeze spell**
Any disruptive element (bill, eviction notice, conflict) — write "paid in full" or desired outcome, fold away from you, submerge in water in freezer. Stops energetic drain while you build.

**Tool: Road opener candle**
Orange or yellow tea light (or spell candle). Carve "OPEN MY ROADS" from bottom up. Anoint with oil. Dress with sugar, cinnamon, coffee. Burn in money bowl. Results: movement in 5-7 days.

**Tool: Blockbuster candle**
Black or white tea light. Carve "CLEAR MY PATHS / REMOVE NEGATIVE BLOCKAGES." Same dressing. Burn same session as road opener, or back to back.
