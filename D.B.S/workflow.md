# WF_DBS_HEALTH_RESEARCH
**D.B.S — Department of Holistic Body Sovereignty & Bodily Autonomy**
**Workflow: From Health Question to Sovereign Protocol**

---

## WHAT THIS WORKFLOW IS

The process for investigating any health topic, herb, nutrient, or body-related question and translating findings into a practical, evidence-honest protocol. Used for both personal health decisions and the Sacred Herb Library.

---

## TRIGGER

- A health question or concern (personal or research-driven)
- A new herb, supplement, or protocol to evaluate
- A health claim encountered that needs evidence evaluation
- A women's health topic flagged for investigation
- A nutritional question or food sovereignty inquiry
- A body symptom that needs understanding, not just treatment

---

## THE WORKFLOW — STEP BY STEP

### STAGE 1 — QUESTION (Nigredo)
**State the health question clearly.**

```
ACTION:
- What specifically is being asked?
- What is the current understanding or practice?
- What prompted this question?
- Is there an existing belief about the answer?
  (State it explicitly — research must be honest,
   not confirmatory)

OUTPUT: dbs_question_[topic]_raw.md
```

---

### STAGE 2 — RESEARCH (Albedo)
**Investigate through D.R.D standards.**

```
ACTION:
Route through D.R.D research methodology:
- Deconstruct the question into sub-questions
- Identify sources across all tiers
- Extract evidence with full discipline

For herbs specifically:
- Load skill_herb-profiling.md
- Build the full herb profile

For general health topics:
- Load skill_source-evaluation.md
- Evaluate claims across traditional AND clinical evidence

IMPORTANT: Traditional use is honored as tradition.
Clinical evidence is honored as clinical evidence.
They are different types of knowing — do not conflate them.

SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_source-evaluation.md, skill_herb-profiling.md (if herb)
OUTPUT: dbs_research_[topic]_evidence.md
```

---

### STAGE 3 — SYNTHESIS (Citrinitas)
**Build the sovereign position on this health topic.**

```
ACTION:
From the evidence gathered, construct:

1. WHAT IS KNOWN: High-confidence findings
2. WHAT IS PROBABLE: Strong evidence but not definitive
3. WHAT IS TRADITIONAL: Used historically with accumulated
   empirical knowledge but limited formal study
4. WHAT IS SPECULATIVE: Claims with thin or no evidence
5. WHAT IS CONTRAINDICATED: Known risks, interactions, or harms

For herbs: Complete the herb profile
For protocols: Design the practical protocol

SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_framework-synthesis.md
OUTPUT: dbs_synthesis_[topic]_position.md
```

---

### STAGE 4 — PROTOCOL (Rubedo)
**Translate findings into actionable practice.**

```
ACTION:
If the research supports action, design the protocol:
- What specifically to do (dosage, frequency, preparation, duration)
- What to monitor (symptoms, markers, subjective experience)
- What to avoid (contraindications, interactions)
- How long to test before evaluating results
- What outcome signals success or failure

OUTPUT: dbs_protocol_[topic]_v1.md
```

---

### STAGE 5 — TRACK AND REFINE
**Observe results and refine the protocol.**

```
ACTION:
- Follow the protocol as designed
- Log observations at regular intervals
- After the evaluation period, assess:
  Did it work? Partially? Not at all?
- Refine the protocol based on direct experience
- Update the herb profile or health file with lived findings

OUTPUT: Updates to existing protocol and research files
```
