# WF_DSE_CLIENT_ENGAGEMENT
**D.S.E — Department of Sovereign Entrepreneurship**
**Workflow: From Opportunity to Sovereign Delivery**

---

## WHAT THIS WORKFLOW IS

The full lifecycle of a business engagement — from the moment an opportunity or client appears to the delivery of value and the closing of the loop. This workflow governs both client services and new business ventures launched from within the OS.

---

## TRIGGER

- A new client inquiry or referral
- A business opportunity identified through research or network
- An internal project with revenue potential
- A product or offer being developed for market

---

## INPUT REQUIRED

```
OPPORTUNITY: What is it? (client engagement / new offer / venture)
SOURCE: Where did this come from?
VALUE PROPOSITION: What value does Morph deliver here?
REVENUE MODEL: How does this generate income?
TIMELINE: When does this need to move?
```

---

## THE WORKFLOW — STEP BY STEP

### STAGE 1 — INTAKE (Nigredo)
**Receive the opportunity without committing.**

```
ACTION:
Document the opportunity exactly as it presents itself.
- What does the client need (in their words)?
- What is the business opportunity (stated plainly)?
- What context and data are available?
Do not evaluate yet. Just receive and document.

OUTPUT: Intake document — dse_intake_[name]_raw.md
```

---

### STAGE 2 — EVALUATION (Albedo)
**Test the opportunity against the sovereign standard.**

```
ACTION — The four evaluation tests:

1. ALIGNMENT: Does this serve the Pandora Empire mission?
   Does it connect to the Triple Helix or long-term vision?

2. SOVEREIGNTY CHECK: Does this expand or contract freedom?
   Does it create dependency on any single client or partner?

3. VALUE CLARITY: Can the value Morph delivers be clearly stated?
   If the value proposition is unclear — the engagement will be too.

4. RESOURCE REALITY: What does this actually cost in time,
   energy, and attention? What gets displaced?

SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_deal-analysis.md
OUTPUT: Evaluation — dse_eval_[name].md
```

**EVALUATION GATE:** Must pass alignment and sovereignty checks to proceed.

---

### STAGE 3 — ARCHITECTURE (Citrinitas)
**Design the offer, deliverable, or business structure.**

```
ACTION:
Build the structure of the engagement:
- What exactly is being delivered?
- What is the price / revenue model?
- What are the terms and boundaries?
- What does the client receive and in what format?
- What is the timeline?
- What does Morph need from the client to deliver?

For new ventures:
- Business model canvas or equivalent
- Minimum viable version
- Phase 1 deliverables

SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_system-design.md, skill_client-transmutation.md
OUTPUT: Engagement architecture — dse_architecture_[name]_v1.md
```

---

### STAGE 4 — DELIVERY (Rubedo)
**Execute and deliver the value.**

```
ACTION:
Execute against the architecture from Stage 3.
- Deliver what was promised, on the terms that were set
- If scope changes during delivery — renegotiate, do not absorb silently
- Document everything produced for the engagement

SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_client-transmutation.md, skill_copywriting.md (if deliverables are written)
OUTPUT: Completed deliverables — dse_delivery_[name]_final.md
```

---

### STAGE 5 — CLOSE AND EXTRACT
**Close the loop and capture what was learned.**

```
ACTION:
1. Confirm delivery is complete and client/customer is served
2. Collect payment if outstanding
3. Extract lessons:
   - What worked well in this engagement?
   - What would be done differently?
   - What reusable assets were created? (templates, frameworks, SOPs)
   - Does this inform a repeatable offer?
4. File everything in D.S.E
5. Cross-link to any department that contributed
   (D.R.D research, D.C.E content, D.I.I tech)

OUTPUT: Engagement record — dse_record_[name]_complete.md
```

## FAILURE MODES TO AVOID

```
COMMITTING BEFORE EVALUATING
Saying yes to an opportunity before testing it
against sovereignty and alignment. Enthusiasm is not evaluation.

UNCLEAR VALUE DELIVERY
Entering an engagement without a specific, articulable
deliverable. "I'll help with your business" is not an offer.

SCOPE ABSORPTION
Accepting scope changes without renegotiating terms.
This contracts sovereignty and trains clients to expect free expansion.

NO EXTRACTION
Completing an engagement and moving on without
capturing what was learned. The engagement data
is as valuable as the revenue.
```
