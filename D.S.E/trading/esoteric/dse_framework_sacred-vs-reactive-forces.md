## SECTION 1 — THE TWO CATEGORIES OF PLANETARY FORCE

### THE SACRED PLANETS (Transmuting Forces)
**Vulcan, Mercury (higher octave), Venus (higher octave), Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, Pluto**

In Bailey's system, the sacred planets are those that have fully expressed their potential as individualized planetary beings and now operate as transmitting agencies for higher solar energies. In markets, their transits produce:

- Genuine regime changes (not just corrections within an existing regime)
- Structural transformation of established systems
- The emergence of a new dominant force that cannot be reversed
- Events whose consequences unfold over years or decades

**The practical test:** If the market conditions produced by a planetary transit are still structurally present 5 years later — it was a sacred (transmuting) event.

### THE NON-SACRED (REACTIVE) PLANETS
**The Moon, the Sun, Mars, and the personality-level expressions of Mercury and Venus**

The reactive forces govern personality-level dynamics: the daily and weekly oscillations, the news-driven reactions, the emotional swings of the collective. They create:

- Swing cycles (bearish for days/weeks, then reverting)
- Data-driven price reactions (NFP, CPI, FOMC — the information ceremonies)
- The session-level psychology (fear, greed, FOMO, capitulation within a single week)
- Patterns that LOOK like new trends but are actually reactions within the existing structure

**The practical test:** If market conditions produced by a transit have reversed or normalized within 6 months — it was a reactive event within an existing structure.

## SECTION 3 — THE REACTIVE FORCES: MARKET EXPRESSIONS

### THE MOON (Daily / Weekly Cycle)
The Moon governs the emotional tides of the collective consciousness. Its 29.5-day cycle moves through every market.

**Practical Moon framework:**
```
NEW MOON: Market initiating new energy. Watch for the setup that will play out over 2 weeks.
WAXING (New → Full): Momentum building. Bullish environments tend to expand during waxing.
FULL MOON: Maximum tension. Emotional peaks. Reversals often occur within 2-3 days.
WANING (Full → New): Releasing energy. Consolidations and retracement during waning.
```

**High-precision Moon signals:**
- Moon conjunct a key planetary position (Mars, Jupiter, Saturn) = spike in that energy for 24-48 hours
- Moon in the sign ruling the dominant market sector = heightened activity in that sector
- Void-of-Course Moon (Moon in transition between signs): typically flat, low-energy markets — avoid new positions

**Current (May 2026):** Cancer stellium amplifies Moon's impact — when the Moon transits through Cancer and conjuncts Jupiter/Venus, the gold + safe-haven energy amplifies significantly for 24-48 hours.
