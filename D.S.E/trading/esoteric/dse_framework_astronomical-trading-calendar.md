## SECTION 6 — THE LUNAR CYCLE TRADING PROTOCOL

The Moon completes a cycle every 29.5 days. This is the most operationally immediate astrological cycle.

### The Eight Phases

| Phase | Duration | Market Tendency | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **New Moon** | Day 0-1 | New initiation energy. New trends begin here OR existing trends accelerate. | Watch for breakouts and new directional moves. |
| **Waxing Crescent** | Days 2-6 | Building energy. Trends that began at New Moon develop. | Add to positions if trend is confirmed. |
| **First Quarter** | Day 7-8 | First inflection point. A "test" of the new direction. | Watch for brief counter-move then resumption. |
| **Waxing Gibbous** | Days 9-13 | Momentum phase. Trends accelerate. | Maximum momentum window — trend trades. |
| **Full Moon** | Day 14-15 | Culmination. Peak energy. Highest probability reversal point. | Look for exhaustion signals. Often: the high/low of the short-term swing. |
| **Waning Gibbous** | Days 16-20 | Momentum fades. Distribution begins. | Begin reducing long positions if at a high; begin covering shorts if at a low. |
| **Last Quarter** | Day 21-22 | Second inflection. Declining energy. | Watch for acceleration in the reversing direction. |
| **Waning Crescent** | Days 23-28 | Energy depletes. Markets consolidate. | Reduce exposure. Prepare for next New Moon cycle. |

### Applying the Lunar Cycle to STIS

*Before running any trade: note the current lunar phase.*

- If you are in Waxing (New Moon through Full Moon) → bias toward trend-following entries
- If you are in Waning (Full Moon through next New Moon) → bias toward counter-trend setups and exits
- Never open a new major position within 2 days of a Full Moon or New Moon without extraordinary conviction

```
30 days  → First natural reaction / retest
45 days  → Secondary correction or continuation signal
60 days  → Trend change warning — if trend holds past 60, strong trend
90 days  → Quarter cycle — major support/resistance in time
120 days → Midpoint of annual cycle — often the largest correction in a bull run
144 days → The "natural" year (12 × 12 = 144° of the circle)
180 days → Half-year turning point — equal time to the first half
270 days → Three-quarter cycle — setup for the annual resolution
360 days → Full cycle — anniversary of the major turning point
```

**Application:** Identify the last major high or low. Count forward to each Gann interval. Mark those dates on your trading calendar. They are potential reversal or acceleration dates — which one they are depends on the chart structure and what the esoteric layer (this file) says about the energetic conditions at that time.
