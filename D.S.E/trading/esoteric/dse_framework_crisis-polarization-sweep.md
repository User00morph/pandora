## SECTION 1 — THE LAW ITSELF

From Alice Bailey, applied universally: all cyclic change moves through three phases.

```
CRISIS
  The existing equilibrium breaks.
  Tension exceeds the structure's capacity to hold it.
  Volatility expands. Consensus breaks.
  Narrative becomes incoherent — multiple contradictory stories compete.
  This is the most dangerous phase for undisciplined capital.
  The Mutable Cross is in maximum pain or maximum confusion.
  The Fixed Cross is being forced to unwind positions it held through the prior Sweep.
  The Cardinal Cross: OBSERVES. Does not trade. Takes no new positions.
  Crisis is not the entry. It is the prerequisite for the entry.
        ↓
POLARIZATION
  From the chaos of Crisis, a new dominant force emerges.
  One side begins to win decisively.
  A new structure begins to form — tentatively at first.
  New narrative establishes. New leaders emerge.
  The CHoCH (Change of Character) is the structural signal that Polarization is underway.
  The Cardinal Cross: CALIBRATES. Identifies the new dominant Ray. Prepares the hypothesis.
  Polarization is the confirmation window — the market is showing who won the Crisis.
        ↓
SWEEP FORWARD
  The new force expresses unimpeded.
  Trend is clean. Structure is making HH+HL (or LL+LH in a downtrend).
  Volume is consistent. Price moves with minimal friction.
  The dominant Ray is expressing at full strength.
  The Cardinal Cross: EXECUTES. Positions in the direction of the Sweep with defined risk.
  The Sweep is where actionable edge lives. The objective is to be positioned at the START of the Sweep — not in the middle, not chasing it at the end.
        ↓
[A new CRISIS begins as the Sweep exhausts itself and the next tension builds]
```

## SECTION 2 — IDENTIFYING EACH PHASE IN PRICE

### Identifying the CRISIS Phase

**Price structure signals:**
- Range is breaking in unpredictable directions (whipsaws)
- BOS and CHoCH are happening rapidly on multiple timeframes — no sustained direction
- Wicks are extremely large (Mutable Cross panic in both directions)
- Gaps forming and being filled rapidly
- Volume is elevated and erratic — not sustained in one direction

**Data layer signals:**
- VIX elevated and rising
- FRA-OIS spread widening
- SOFR spiking above Fed Funds Rate
- COT: all three groups moving rapidly (unusual repositioning)
- News cycle: contradictory headlines, no clear dominant narrative

**Astronomical layer signals:**
- Multiple planets stationing (retrograde or direct) simultaneously
- Eclipse within 6 weeks (before or after)
- Mars conjunct an outer planet (Uranus, Saturn, Pluto)
- A significant aspect becoming exact (tight orb, within 1°)

**Sovereign action:** REDUCE ALL POSITIONS TO MINIMUM. Move to observation. Log everything. The Crisis is generating the data that will define the next Polarization direction.

### Identifying the POLARIZATION Phase

**Price structure signals:**
- A clear CHoCH has occurred on the daily or 4H chart
- Price is beginning to make a new pattern (HL forming after a downtrend, or LH forming after an uptrend)
- The first tentative BOS in the new direction has occurred
- Structure is not yet clean — pullbacks are still relatively large

**Data layer signals:**
- VIX is stabilizing or beginning to fall
- One directional bias is beginning to dominate in the COT data
- DXY is beginning to show a directional lean (not just ranging)
- The intermarket chain is aligning — two or three asset classes confirming the new direction

**Astronomical layer signals:**
- A planet has stationed (gone direct or retrograde) and the energy is stabilizing
- The eclipse window has passed (eclipses create the Crisis; the window after = Polarization begins)
- The dominant aspect is separating (past exact — its energy is resolved, not building)
- A new planetary ingress has just occurred (a new Ray beginning to express)

**Sovereign action:** CALIBRATE TO THE NEW RAY. Map the new structure. Define conditions for the entry — specifically what price must do to confirm the Polarization has become a Sweep. Do not enter yet. Wait for the Sweep confirmation.

**The Polarization entry signal (the highest-probability entry in the entire cycle):**
A liquidity sweep into the new structural low/high (one last Crisis-remnant stop hunt), followed by an immediate recovery and a clean BOS in the Polarization direction. This is the end of Polarization → beginning of Sweep transition — the highest-probability, highest R:R entry in the system.

### Identifying the SWEEP Phase

**Price structure signals:**
- Clean HH+HL (uptrend) or LL+LH (downtrend) on the active timeframe
- Pullbacks are shallow (30-40% of the prior swing, staying in the discount zone for longs)
- BOS candles are strong and sustained (not wicked and reversed)
- Volume on impulses is higher than volume on pullbacks

**Data layer signals:**
- VIX is falling (risk improving)
- COT: Commercial Hedgers positioned in the Sweep direction at a sustained level (not at an extreme yet)
- DXY is trending clearly in the direction that supports the Sweep
- Intermarket chain fully aligned with the Sweep direction

**Astronomical layer signals:**
- The dominant Ray is expressing cleanly through the relevant pair
- No major planetary stations within 3 weeks
- No eclipse within 6 weeks
- Moon phase is waxing (if the Sweep is upward)

**Sovereign action:** EXECUTE WITHIN THE SWEEP. Enter on pullbacks to Order Blocks, FVGs, and structural lows. Size appropriately. Ride the Sweep until structural signals suggest its exhaustion.

**Sweep exhaustion signals (the next Crisis approaching):**
- Impulse candles becoming progressively smaller
- Pullbacks becoming progressively deeper
- Volume declining on new highs/lows (declining momentum)
- COT approaching a historical extreme in the direction of the Sweep
- An astronomical event (eclipse, major conjunction) within 6 weeks
- A major economic event (FOMC with regime change language)

**Sovereign action at Sweep exhaustion:** Begin reducing position size. Tighten stops. Take partial profits. Do not add new positions. Prepare for the next Crisis.

## SECTION 3 — THE FRACTAL NATURE OF THE CYCLE

The C→P→S cycle plays out on EVERY timeframe simultaneously. The same structure exists on a 5-minute chart as on a monthly chart. The difference is only scale.

```
MONTHLY CHART:   One complete C→P→S cycle = 12-36 months
WEEKLY CHART:    One cycle within the monthly = 4-12 weeks
DAILY CHART:     One cycle within the weekly = 5-20 days
4H CHART:        One cycle within the daily = 1-5 days
1H CHART:        One cycle within the 4H = 4-24 hours
15M CHART:       One cycle within the 1H = 30-240 minutes
```

**The multi-timeframe rule:** When C→P→S cycles align across multiple timeframes, the move is of maximum power. A Sweep on the daily + weekly + monthly all aligned = the highest-conviction directional trade. A Sweep on the 15m but a Crisis on the daily = trade the 15m Sweep with minimal size.

**The nesting principle:** A Crisis on the 1H is usually just a Polarization or Sweep correction on the 4H. Before declaring Crisis on any timeframe, check: is this just a pullback within the higher timeframe Sweep?
