### PATTERN 1 — THE LIQUIDITY SWEEP + REVERSAL
**Classification:** High probability | All timeframes | All pairs

**Structure:**
```
─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ Key Level (equal lows / swing low / round number) ─ ─ ─
                    ↓ spike below (the sweep)
                   LOW ← wicks below the level (long lower wick)
                    ↑ immediate recovery
─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ Key Level ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─
                    ↑ continuation above
```

**Consciousness read:** The Mutable Cross was positioned short below the key level (stops + short entries). The Cardinal Cross used this liquidity pool to fill long positions. The sweep triggered retail stops (their sell orders = institutional buy orders). The immediate reversal = institutional accumulation complete.

**Ray signature:** R1 (Will/Power) — a decisive assertion of directional intent. The sweep is R1 asserting through R7's ceremony (the obvious level that everyone is watching).

**Entry protocol:**
1. Price sweeps below the key level
2. A rejection candle forms (hammer, pin bar, engulfing) back above the level
3. Enter on close of the rejection candle or on the next candle's open
4. Stop: below the wick of the sweep candle (below where price went and was rejected)
5. Target: the opposing liquidity pool (equal highs or previous swing high)

**Confirming signals:**
- Daily bias is LONG (structure confirmed from higher timeframe)
- Sweep occurred in a discount zone (below the 50% of the recent swing)
- COT shows Commercial Hedgers net long at an extreme
- Lunar cycle is waxing (momentum phase)
- No major economic event within 4 hours

### PATTERN 2 — THE ORDER BLOCK RETURN
**Classification:** High probability | 1H-4H primary | Major pairs

**Structure:**
```
[Last bearish candle] ← ORDER BLOCK ZONE
        ↓
[Strong bullish impulse] ← price leaves the OB and moves strongly up
        ↑
[Pullback returns to OB zone] ← price returns to where it launched from
        ↑
[Rejection at OB / continuation up] ← OB holds, new impulse begins
```

**Consciousness read:** The Order Block is where the Cardinal Cross made its decision. When price returns, the Fixed Cross (trend followers who missed the first move) enters at the decision zone. The Cardinal Cross defends the zone. The Mutable Cross (who sold the pullback, expecting a reversal) is stopped out as price resumes.

**Ray signature:** R2 (Love/Wisdom) — the accumulative, attractive return to the zone of value. R2 moves draw price back to equilibrium before resuming the R1 impulse.

**Entry protocol:**
1. Identify the last opposing candle before a strong impulse (the Order Block)
2. Mark the OB zone: high and low of that candle
3. Wait for price to return to the OB zone on a pullback
4. Enter at the 50-61.8% level within the OB zone (the premium within the discount)
5. Stop: below the bottom of the OB zone (with a 5-pip buffer)
6. Target: the previous impulse high, then the opposing liquidity pool

**Confirming signals:**
- The OB aligns with a higher timeframe support level
- The pullback into the OB is on lower volume than the impulse
- A rejection candle (pin bar, engulfing) forms within the OB zone
- The FVG left by the impulse has not yet been filled (the OB and FVG are nearby)
- Structure is still making HH+HL (uptrend intact)

### PATTERN 3 — THE FAIR VALUE GAP FILL + REJECTION
**Classification:** High probability | 15m-1H primary | All pairs

**Structure:**
```

Candle 2: ═══════ (large impulse candle — creates the gap)
            [FVG ZONE]
Candle 3: ─────── (opens above C1 high, the gap is left)
                ↑
[Price moves up, then returns to fill the FVG]
                ↓
[FVG acts as support — rejection candle forms in FVG]
                ↑
[Continuation in impulse direction]
```

**Consciousness read:** The FVG is an unresolved imbalance — a moment where collective consciousness moved so fast that it left an unfilled emotional need (to trade at those prices). **The FVG midpoint rule:** The highest-probability reaction point within the FVG is the 50% level of the gap — the equilibrium of the imbalance.

1. Identify the FVG: candle 1 high, candle 3 low
2. Mark the midpoint: (C1 high + C3 low) / 2
3. Wait for price to return to the FVG zone
4. Enter at or near the midpoint when a rejection candle forms
5. Stop: below the bottom of the FVG (full gap invalidation)

### PATTERN 4 — THE ASIAN RANGE SWEEP + LONDON CONTINUATION
**Classification:** High probability | 15m-1H | Major pairs (especially GBP and EUR)

**Structure:**
```
ASIAN SESSION (7PM-4AM EST):
[price ranges] [price ranges] → establishes Asian High and Asian Low

LONDON OPEN (3AM-5AM EST):
   ↓ price sweeps BELOW the Asian Low (the manipulation)
   ↓ takes the retail sell stops + long exits below the Asian Low
   ↑ immediate reversal ABOVE the Asian Low
   ↑ London then rallies, targeting the Asian High and beyond
```

**Consciousness read:** The Asian session is the range where the Mutable Cross sets its stops (above the high, below the low). London is where the Fixed + Cardinal Cross (institutions) sweep one side of the range, harvest the Mutable Cross liquidity, then continue in the intended direction. The Asian range IS the inducement. London's sweep is the manipulation. The post-sweep move is the real distribution.

**Which side gets swept?** In an overall uptrend: London sweeps the LOWS (takes sell stops below Asian Low) then rallies. In an overall downtrend: London sweeps the HIGHS (takes buy stops above Asian High) then falls.

**Entry protocol:**
1. Before London open: identify Asian High and Asian Low
2. Determine daily bias (from higher timeframe structure)
3. If bullish bias: watch for a sweep below the Asian Low in the 3-5 AM EST window
4. Entry: on the rejection candle that closes back above the Asian Low
5. Stop: below the sweep candle's wick
6. Target: Asian High, then previous day's high

**The timing confirmation:** If a sweep of the Asian Low happens AND the daily bias is bullish AND there is a bullish FVG or OB nearby → this is one of the highest probability setups in the forex playbook.

### PATTERN 5 — THE DOUBLE TOP / DOUBLE BOTTOM (EQUAL HIGHS/LOWS TRAP)
**Classification:** Medium-high probability | 1H-Daily | All instruments

**Structure (bearish — Double Top):**
```
         HIGH 1          HIGH 2 (equal to HIGH 1)
        /      \        /      \
       /        \      /        ↓ break below the middle low (the "neckline")
      /          \    /         ↓ CONTINUATION DOWN (after neckline break)
```

**Why retail gets this wrong:** Most traders wait for the neckline break to enter short. By the time the neckline breaks, they are entering at a poor risk-to-reward location. The Sovereign enters differently.

**The Sovereign's entry:**
1. Price makes Equal High 2 (a liquidity sweep above High 1, then rejection)
2. The sweep of EH1 into EH2 = the trap. Retail breakout buyers get swept.
3. The rejection at EH2 = entry short (with stop above EH2 wick)
4. Target: the neckline, then the measured move below (height of the pattern)

**The equal highs are the target, not the resistance.** Institutions push price to equal highs to sweep the buy stops clustered above EH1 before selling. The entry is AFTER the sweep, not before it.

**Consciousness read:** R4 (Harmony through Conflict) — the two peaks are the conflict polarization. The resolution is the collapse through the neckline. The Sovereign reads the conflict pattern and positions for the resolution BEFORE it fully manifests.

4. Enter short when a rejection candle forms at the Breaker zone

### PATTERN 7 — THE COMPRESSION + EXPANSION (THE COILING SPRING)
**Classification:** High probability timing | All timeframes | Most powerful on Daily

**Structure:**
```
[Normal candle] [Normal candle] [Smaller] [Smaller] [Tiny — inside bar] → EXPANSION
                                                                              ↑ or ↓
```

**Consciousness read:** The compression is the Polarization phase. Two forces are in equilibrium. The market is "deciding." Volume falls. Ranges narrow. The collective is holding its breath. This is the most deceptive phase — it appears that "nothing is happening" — but it is the moment of maximum energy storage. The expansion that follows is the Sweep phase — powerful, directional, sustained.

**The Sovereign's position during compression:** Observe. Note the structure context. Determine which direction the expansion is more likely given:
- Higher timeframe bias
- COT positioning
- Upcoming catalyst (FOMC, NFP, eclipse window)
- The Ray signature of the current period

**Entry protocol:**
1. Identify the compression: 3+ inside bars or progressively smaller candles
2. Do NOT enter during the compression. Wait for the expansion candle.
3. Enter on the CLOSE of the first large expansion candle (in the direction of the break)
4. Stop: below the compression range (for long) or above (for short)
5. Target: the next significant structural level in the expansion direction

**The false expansion:** Sometimes price breaks out of compression and immediately reverses (the inducement). The tell: the "expansion" candle has a very long wick and closes back inside the compression range. This is NOT the real expansion — it is the manipulation. Wait for the second candle to confirm.

### PATTERN 8 — THE VOLUME CLIMAX REVERSAL
**Classification:** High probability reversal signal | Daily / Weekly primary

**Structure:**
```
[Sustained trend down: 5-10+ candles]
                                    ↓
[CLIMAX CANDLE: largest bearish candle of the entire trend, highest volume]
                                    ↓
[Immediate recovery — smaller candle, closes with bullish body]
                                    ↑ REVERSAL CONFIRMED
```

**Consciousness read:** The climax candle is maximum Mutable Cross capitulation. Every person who was going to sell has now sold. The fear is maximum. The volume is the entire accumulated emotional pressure releasing at once. After maximum capitulation, there is no one left to sell — so price recovers. The institutional buyer has been waiting for exactly this moment — they are the ones absorbing the panicked selling at maximum volume.

**Why volume is the key:** Without the volume spike, it is just another bearish candle. The volume spike confirms that this is not ordinary selling — it is the final exhaustion of all sellers. The emotional energy of the move has been spent.

**Entry protocol:**
1. Identify a sustained downtrend (minimum 5 candles)
2. The climax candle: significantly larger body + significantly higher volume than prior candles
3. The recovery candle: closes bullish after the climax
4. Enter long on the close of the recovery candle or the next open
5. Stop: below the wick of the climax candle
6. Target: the middle of the prior trend (50% retracement), then the origin of the trend

**The Ray signature:** R4 (Harmony through Conflict) — the maximum conflict produces the maximum resolution. The climax candle IS the crisis phase. The recovery IS the polarization. The new trend is the new Sweep.
