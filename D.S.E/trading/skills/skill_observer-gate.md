# SKILL — Observer Gate (L5 Pre-Execution Check)
**Nervous system sovereignty protocol | Must pass before any trade or analysis**
**Load when:** At the START of every session and before any execution decision.

---

## WHAT THIS IS

The Observer Gate is not a ritual. It is a pre-condition. The nervous system is the instrument — and an instrument in a reactive state produces distorted readings. A distorted reading from a Grade A setup produces a Grade A loss.

**Rule:** If the Observer Gate is BLOCKED → no trade, no matter what the chart shows. Log it. Return later.

**The inverse is also true:** An Observer in sovereign state with no setup > an Observer in reactive state with a perfect chart.

---

## THE 5 PRE-SESSION QUESTIONS

Answer each honestly. A forced "yes" is a program signal.

```
1. Am I present to this moment — or am I in a story about what happened yesterday / 
   what might happen tomorrow?
   
   CLEAR: I am here now. The past trade is complete. The future is unknown.
   BLOCKED: Replaying a loss | Imagining a win | Projecting yesterday onto today.

2. Am I making this decision from desire to participate — or from desire to recover?
   
   CLEAR: I trade only when the setup is Grade A. I am not "owed" a trade.
   BLOCKED: I need to make back what I lost | I need to prove I was right | I've been waiting
            all week and I need to trade today.

3. Am I reading the chart — or am I reading what I want to see in the chart?
   
   CLEAR: I am following the 5-layer protocol. The chart tells me. I do not tell the chart.
   BLOCKED: I decided the direction before opening the chart | I'm finding evidence for a 
            pre-existing bias | I'm ignoring data that contradicts my read.

4. Am I in the body — or in the head?
   
   CLEAR: Breathing is normal. No tension in chest or jaw. No urgency.
   BLOCKED: Heart rate elevated without physical cause | Scanning charts faster than reading
            them | Feeling like I'm going to miss something | Tightness in chest/shoulders.

5. Am I willing to not trade today if no Grade A setup appears?
   
   CLEAR: Yes. Cash is a position. Observation is work.
   BLOCKED: No — I need to trade | I'll find something | I can make one exception.
```

---

## PROGRAM SIGNAL DETECTION

These states are not character flaws — they are programs running. Identify them without judgment, and exit the session.

| Signal | What It Feels Like | What It Sounds Like |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Urgency** | Can't wait | "I need to get in now before it moves" |
| **Justification** | Finding reasons | "Well, 5 of 7 criteria is basically 7 of 7" |
| **Revenge** | Body tension, focus on recovering loss | "I just need one good trade to get back to even" |
| **Entitlement** | Impatience | "I've been patient all week, I deserve a setup" |
| **Certainty** | Overconfidence | "This is definitely going up, I don't need to run the filter" |
| **Fear of missing** | Scanning frantically | "It just moved 50 pips without me, I need to catch the next one" |
| **Doubt spiral** | Second-guessing | Changing analysis every 5 minutes on the same chart |

**Any single program signal = BLOCKED. Leave the session.**

## OBSERVER SIGNALS (CLEAR STATE)

| Signal | What It Feels Like |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Stillness** | No urgency. Comfortable with not knowing. |
| **Curiosity** | Interested in what the chart shows, not invested in what it should show |
| **Detachment** | Reading the setup like reading a field report — not like money is on the line |
| **Patience** | Willing to close the terminal and return later |
| **Acceptance** | No resistance to "nothing to trade today" |

## THE OBSERVER FLIP PROTOCOL

If you arrive at the session BLOCKED — do not force entry into the Clear state. The Observer Flip is not willpower. It is a perspective shift.

**The Flip:** Stop looking AT the chart. Step back to the position that is LOOKING through you.

Practical: close the terminal. Set a 20-minute timer. Do anything physical (walk, breathe, move). Return. Re-run the 5 questions. If still blocked — no session today.

**There is no such thing as missing a trade. There is only: was I present to read the field?**

