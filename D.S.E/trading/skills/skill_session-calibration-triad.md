## PURPOSE

The Session Triad is the three-question protocol from the Observer Calibration — expanded here into a full skill. The three questions (Session Sun, Session Rising, Session Moon) provide the Sovereign's reading of the current market's consciousness state before any chart is opened.

Unlike the esoteric reading (which takes 30+ minutes for a full reading), the Triad is rapid (5 minutes) and cuts directly to the narrative intelligence layer — identifying what the market thinks it is doing vs what it is actually doing vs what it is no longer doing but hasn't realized yet.

---

## THE THREE QUESTIONS

### SESSION SUN — The Present Problem
**Question:** "What is the dominant question the market is trying to solve today?"

This is the SURFACE LEVEL — what the market's collective consciousness is oriented around right now. What problem is the media discussing? What data point is the market waiting for? What concern is dominating the financial discourse?

**Format:** One declarative sentence.
**Example:** "The dominant question today is whether CPI data releases in line with estimates or surprises on the upside, which would delay any Fed cut expectations."

**What it is NOT:** An opinion about direction. A prediction. It is simply naming the problem the collective is focused on.

---

### SESSION RISING — The Actual Field
**Question:** "What is actually being brought forward beneath the headline narrative?"

This is the DEPTH LAYER — what the intermarket data, positioning, and esoteric cycle are actually expressing beneath the surface narrative. The Rising is what is RISING INTO VISIBILITY even if the surface narrative hasn't named it yet.

The Rising is identified by checking:
- What is the intermarket chain showing? (bonds, yields, DXY, gold moving in what direction?)
- What does the COT positioning reveal about where smart money actually sits?
- What is the current Ray expression beneath the narrative?

**Format:** One declarative sentence.
**Example:** "The actual field rising is the structural fiscal stress signal: the 30-year yield at 5.20% is showing that the bond market doesn't believe in fiscal sustainability — this is the real constraint on any soft-landing story."

---

### SESSION MOON — The Dead Narrative
**Question:** "What story is everyone still trading that has already expired?"

This is the LAGGING LAYER — the narrative the crowd is still carrying that the field has already moved past. The Moon is the dead light of a star that has already changed. The crowd is still oriented around a story that the field has contradicted.

Identifying the Moon:
- What was the dominant narrative 1-3 months ago that is now contradicted by data?
- What does the retail sentiment indicator show (extreme positioning based on an old narrative)?
- What "obvious" story is priced in but may no longer reflect current reality?

**Format:** One declarative sentence.
**Example:** "The dead narrative being carried is 'soft landing is assured' — the bond market, the 18-year high on 30-year yields, and the TIC data showing foreign official selling have all already told a different story. The equity market is the last to receive the memo."

## THE TRIAD IN PRACTICE

### Full Execution (5 minutes)

```
SESSION SUN:
Check: financial media headlines, pre-market economic calendar
"The dominant question today is: _______________"

SESSION RISING:
Check: DXY direction on daily | 10-year yield: up/down/flat | Gold: up/down/flat | 
       Last COT read | Dominant Ray | Any overnight market movement
"Beneath the headline, what is actually expressing: _______________"

SESSION MOON:
Check: What was the narrative consensus 4-8 weeks ago? Has it been contradicted?
       Retail sentiment: is positioning based on an old story?
"The expired story still being traded: _______________"
```

## TRIAD INTERPRETATION: WHAT THE COMBINATIONS MEAN

### Sun and Rising ALIGNED:
The surface narrative matches the field truth. High-conviction sessions — what the crowd discusses IS what the field is doing. These are the cleaner sessions for directional trading.

### Sun CONTRADICTS Rising (most common):
The crowd is oriented around one problem (the Sun) but the field is actually expressing something else (the Rising). This is where the edge lives — the gap between surface and depth. The trade is taken in the direction of the Rising, not the Sun.

**Example:** 
- Sun: "Market waiting for CPI to decide if rates go higher"
- Rising: "Gold at ATH, foreign central banks selling Treasuries, 30-year at 18-year high — the dollar credibility question is the real field"
- Trade implication: Gold long — the Rising narrative (dollar credibility) supports it even if CPI disappoints

### Sun and Moon are THE SAME:
The "current problem" and the "expired story" are identical. This is a Phase 4-5 narrative signal — the crowd is so immersed in the dominant story that it has become the only story. Maximum contrarian signal.

**Example:** Sun = "soft landing confirmed" / Moon = "soft landing assured" → these are the same story. The market has fully priced this in. The contrarian position (against the soft landing) has better R:R.

### Rising CONTRADICTS Moon:
The actual field is expressing something that directly contradicts the dead narrative. This is the Phase 5 collapse signal — the Rising is the new reality, the Moon is what the crowd still holds. The convergence event will force the Moon narrative to collapse into the Rising reality.

## TRIAD EXAMPLES — CURRENT MARKET (MAY 2026)

```
SESSION SUN:
"The dominant question is whether the Fed signals more rate pauses or hints at cuts given 
moderating but sticky inflation — the market is oriented around the next FOMC's language."

SESSION RISING:
"What is actually rising is the fiscal sustainability concern: the 30-year yield at 5.20% (18-year 
high) with $6.7T Fed balance sheet flat suggests the bond market is repricing US fiscal risk, not 
just monetary policy — this is the deeper constraint the equity market has not yet priced."

SESSION MOON:
"The expired story is that 'inflation is conquered and soft landing is the base case' — the bond 
market, TIC data showing foreign official selling (-$46.1B Feb 2026), and 30-year yield signal 
have all already contradicted this. The equity market and financial media are the last holders."

TRIAD IMPLICATION:
- Sun = rate timing question (short-term, event-driven)
- Rising = fiscal sustainability (medium-term, structural)
- Moon = soft landing confidence (contradicted by the field)
- Trade: Gold long (benefits from both the fiscal concern in the Rising AND the eventual collapse of 
  the soft landing Moon narrative). Dollar bears are correctly reading the Rising.
```
