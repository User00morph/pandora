## OVERVIEW: THE THREE THEMES OF 2026

The transit calendar for May 12 – December 31, 2026 is structured around three dominant transit threads that run through the year:

1. **Saturn conjunct natal Venus (Aries)** — the relational-value crucible (active May–June 2026)
2. **Neptune conjunct natal N.Node (Aries)** — the evolutionary dissolution (active all year)
3. **Jupiter through Cancer → Leo** — the expansion chapter's primary transit arc (July 2026 onward)

These three threads do not occur in isolation — they weave through each other continuously. The transit calendar is not a list of events but a living intersection of multiple archetypal processes.
