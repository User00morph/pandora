## DOCTRINE

**Zodiacal Releasing** (ZR) is a Hellenistic time-lord technique developed by Vettius Valens (2nd century CE). It operates differently from Firdaria or Vimshottari Dasha — it unfolds directly from the Lots (particularly the Lot of Fortune and Lot of Spirit), measuring life chapters by the zodiacal signs and their assigned planetary-period lengths.

**Starting point:** The Lot of Fortune (for general life periods) or Lot of Spirit (for action and career periods). Each sign receives a fixed number of months according to its planetary lord's minor years (Ptolemy's periods):

| Sign | Lord | Period Length |
|---|---|---|
| Aries | Mars | 15 months |
| Taurus | Venus | 11 months |
| Gemini | Mercury | 20 months |
| Cancer | Moon | 25 months |
| Leo | Sun | 19 months |
| Virgo | Mercury | 20 months |
| Libra | Venus | 8 months |
| Scorpio | Mars | 15 months |
| Sagittarius | Jupiter | 12 months |
| Capricorn | Saturn | 27 months |
| Aquarius | Saturn | 30 months |
| Pisces | Jupiter | 12 months |

**The sequence:** Starting from the sign of the Lot, the periods move through the zodiac in order (Aries → Taurus → Gemini → ...) for the duration of each sign's period, then the next sign begins. After the 12th sign, the sequence restarts from the first sign.

**Peaked periods:** When a ZR period lands on the sign of the Lot itself, or the sign of the Lot's lord, or the 10th from the Lot, it is a "Peaked" period — a chapter of maximum elevation, activation, and peak-life significance.
