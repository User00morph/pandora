## What the Sidereal Chart Is

The **tropical chart** (used by Western astrology) measures signs from the Spring Equinox (0° Aries = March equinox). The **sidereal chart** (used by Vedic/Jyotish astrology) measures signs from the actual positions of constellations against the fixed stars, applying a corrective factor called the ayanamsa.

After ~2000 years of **precession of the equinoxes**, the tropical spring equinox point has shifted ~25° from where the actual Aries constellation begins. The Lahiri ayanamsa (24.8291° for 2006) corrects for this drift.

**The result:** every planet shifts back ~25° in the zodiac. A planet at 25° Taurus tropically becomes ~00° Taurus siderially — or drops into the end of Aries.

**Frame for integration:** Tropical = *what is being expressed in this life (personality layer)*. Sidereal = *what the soul is expressing through the fixed-star matrix (stellar body layer)*. Both are correct. The code-fairy framework (sourced from @astrocodefairy, SNRS Layer 12) holds all three chart types simultaneously.

## KEY SIDEREAL OBSERVATIONS

### Venus in Pisces — Exaltation
In the sidereal chart, **Venus moves from Aries (tropical Detriment) to Pisces (sidereal Exaltation)**. This is a profound inversion of the tropical reading. Where the tropical chart names the detriment — love that is self-directed, that burns fast — the sidereal chart places Venus in her strongest possible sign. The soul's aesthetic and relational frequency is, at the stellar-body level, in exaltation: oceanic, compassionate, boundary-dissolving love. The tropical Aries quality (the test) overlays a sidereal Pisces quality (the gift).

**Integration:** The soul knows how to love with total dissolution of self (Pisces). The current-life curriculum (tropical Aries) is learning to love without dissolving — to maintain self-definition within that oceanic capacity. Both are real; neither erases the other.

### Moon in Libra — Shift from Fall to Neutral
The Scorpio Moon (tropical Fall) becomes a **Libra Moon siderially** — no longer in Fall, now in the sign of balance, partnership, and aesthetic harmony. The Libra Moon seeks equilibrium, relational fairness, and beauty as emotional sustenance.

**Integration:** The tropical Scorpio Moon describes the *intensity* of how this emotional body experiences feeling — the depth, the possessiveness, the drive to go all the way in. The sidereal Libra Moon describes the *underlying desire* beneath that intensity: to be met, to have balance, to find the one who matches. The Scorpio layer is the vehicle; the Libra layer is the destination.

### Sun in Aries — Fire Core Beneath the Taurus Surface
The steady Taurus Sun (tropical) contains a sidereal **Aries Sun** — an initiatory, self-asserting fire core. At the stellar level, this is not a builder-sign Sun but a pioneer-sign Sun. The Aries quality that appears in this chart's Venus (tropical) and N.Node is mirrored in the Sun itself at the sidereal level.

**Integration:** The Taurus persistence and resource-orientation (tropical) is the *style* of expression. The Aries fire (sidereal) is the *root drive*. The native initiates (Aries core) with the sustained energy of Taurus packaging.

### Moon-Jupiter Conjunction Persists (Libra)
Both Moon and Jupiter shift to **Libra** siderially — the conjunction holds (Moon at 18° Libra 06', Jupiter at 18° Libra 10' — a near-exact 0.07° conjunction in Libra). This confirms the Moon-Jupiter conjunction as a fixed soul signature that persists across chart systems. In Libra, the conjunction carries: expansive emotional attunement to relationship, justice, and beauty. Aesthetic amplification. The relational domain as the primary arena of soul experience.

### Saturn in Cancer — Shift from Leo Detriment to Cancer
Saturn moves from **Leo (Detriment, tropical)** to **Cancer (sidereal)**. In Cancer, Saturn's test shifts from heart-power and pride to **security, roots, family, and emotional foundations**. The soul is learning to build with and through emotional material — not despite it. Saturn in Cancer tests whether structure can be built without emotional suppression.

**Integration with tropical:** The tropical Leo Saturn asks whether creative self-expression can be generated from love rather than ego. The sidereal Cancer Saturn asks whether the home-base, the emotional foundation, can be built with real discipline rather than avoidance. Both tests are active.

### Sidereal ASC: Gemini — The Communicator Rising
Where the tropical Cancer ASC presents as receptive, nurturing, and feeling-oriented, the **sidereal Gemini ASC** presents as mercurial, communicative, information-processing, and dualistic. At the stellar-body level, the primary mode of interfacing with the world is through language, connection, exchange of ideas.

**Integration:** The Cancer exterior (feeling, protection, sensitivity) is the *adaptation layer* for this incarnation. Beneath it, the stellar-body presents as Gemini — built to communicate, to synthesize, to bridge. The Gemini ASC feeds into the Mercury sidereal placement in Aries (direct, assertive communication) as the underlying communicative force.
