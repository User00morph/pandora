## DOCTRINE

The Arabic Parts (also called Hellenistic Lots) are sensitive points derived from the arithmetic relationship between two planets and the Ascendant. Each Lot identifies a domain of life experience through the intersection of two planetary principles.

**Night chart formula for Fortune:** ASC + Moon − Sun  
**Day chart formula for Fortune:** ASC + Sun − Moon  

This is a night chart (Sun at 21° Taurus in the 12th Placidus / 11th WS — below the horizon at birth). The night formula is applied for Fortune, and its reversal for Spirit.

**Lot of Fortune** (night): the Lot of the body, material prosperity, and physical vitality  
**Lot of Spirit** (night): the Lot of the soul, action-initiating intelligence, and vocation  

The Lots are not merely symbolic — in Hellenistic practice, the house of Fortune determines the distribution of material fortune across the life; the house of Spirit determines the domain of soulful initiative.
