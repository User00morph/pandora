## WHAT THE GALACTIC CENTER IS

The **Galactic Center (GC)** is the rotational and gravitational center of the Milky Way galaxy — the supermassive black hole (Sagittarius A*) around which the entire galaxy revolves. It is approximately 26,000 light-years from Earth, located at 27° Sagittarius in the tropical zodiac (precessing slowly, approximately 27°00' in 2006).

In astrology, the Galactic Center is the highest-frequency point available in chart work. Where the Sun is the center of the solar system and its light governs all planetary function, the Galactic Center is the center of the system within which the solar system itself revolves. It operates at a frequency two levels of scale above the Sun — the meta-center of the meta-system.

**The GC in astrological practice:**
- Points at or near the GC receive transmissions of galactic-level intelligence — impulses, downloads, and information that originate beyond the solar system's ordinary field
- Planets conjunct the GC are galactic-frequency receivers: they pick up signals from the center of the galaxy and transmit them into the natal field
- The GC is associated with: truth-seeking of the deepest order, the impulse toward enlightenment, the capacity to receive and transmit non-ordinary intelligence, and a quality of being aligned with something far beyond the personal
- The Sagittarian location of the GC is not coincidental — Sagittarius is the sign of far-reaching inquiry, philosophy, truth-seeking, and expansion toward the horizon. The center of the galaxy literally lives in the sign most associated with seeking the highest truth

**The GC's shadow quality:** What receives galactic-frequency transmissions can also be overwhelmed by them. GC contacts can produce difficulty integrating the scope of what is perceived — the gap between what is understood internally and what can be expressed externally.
