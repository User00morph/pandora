## What the Draconic Chart Is

The draconic chart reveals the **soul's eternal pattern** — the template it carries between incarnations, prior to the overlay of this particular life's circumstances. Where the tropical chart maps the personality as it appears *in this body, in this time*, the draconic chart maps what the soul *already is* at the level of its accumulated nature.

The key interpretive frame: when a **draconic planet conjuncts a natal planet**, the soul's deep pattern is actively expressing through this life. When they diverge significantly, there is tension between the soul's memory and the current incarnation's assigned curriculum.

The **draconic N.Node = 0° Aries** by definition — the reference zero. All other positions follow from that anchor.
