## DOCTRINE SUMMARY

The 28 Arabic Lunar Mansions (Manzil al-Qamar) divide the ecliptic into 28 equal portions of 12°51'25.7" each (360° ÷ 28), beginning at 0° Aries. Each mansion is associated with a reference star (or star group), an Arabic name carrying specific archetypal meaning, and a domain of intelligence. In the Western tradition (via Agrippa, Picatrix, and the medieval Islamic astronomers), each mansion governs a specific quality of experience — useful as both a natal placement indicator and a timing tool.

**Calculation method:** Tropical ecliptic longitude / 12.857142...° = mansion number (0-indexed). All positions computed via pyswisseph at birth JD 2453867.046528 (13:07 UT, May 12, 2006).

- The warrior quality of Antares: the one who endures what others cannot because the heart is strong enough to hold it

**Moon in M18 (Al-Qalb):** The emotional intelligence is housed in the Heart mansion. The sect luminary (night chart primary) is in Antares's domain — the soul's emotional core is Antares-quality: sovereign, intense, enduring, oriented toward depth rather than comfort. The feeling intelligence does not flinch at what it encounters; it holds the heart's center steady regardless of what moves through.

**Jupiter in M18 (Al-Qalb):** The wisdom and expansion principle is housed in the same heart-field. The wisdom that Jupiter generates in M18 is hard-won: it has been in the heart of the Scorpion.

**Astraea in M18 (Al-Qalb):** The divine justice principle — already conjunct Jupiter within 4" — is also in the Heart mansion. Astraea's cosmic right order is grounded in the heart's intelligence, not in the mind's. The justice function in this chart is felt before it is reasoned. The body knows before the mind articulates.

**The triple convergence in M18:** Moon + Jupiter + Astraea in Al-Qalb creates a concentrated Antares field in the emotional-wisdom-justice axis. The three most important inner functions of this chart — emotional intelligence (Moon), wisdom and expansion (Jupiter), and divine right order (Astraea) — are all housed in the mansion of the Royal Star of the West, the heart of the Scorpion, Antares.
