## DOCTRINE

Harmonic charts reveal resonance patterns that exist within the natal chart's planetary structure but are not visible in the standard 360° map. Each harmonic divides the circle by a different number — the resulting chart shows which planets share a resonance at that frequency.

**Formula:** H(n) longitude = (natal longitude × n) mod 360°

**Key harmonic meanings:**
- **5th Harmonic (H5):** Creative capacity, talent, artistic intelligence, quintile series resonance
- **7th Harmonic (H7):** Karmic/fate dimensions, deep recurring patterns, septile series resonance
- **9th Harmonic (H9):** Soul purpose, spiritual gift, Navamsa equivalent — the "soul chart" within the natal map

**Conjunction threshold:** Planets within ~2° in a harmonic chart share that harmonic's resonance — they are "in phase" at that frequency.
