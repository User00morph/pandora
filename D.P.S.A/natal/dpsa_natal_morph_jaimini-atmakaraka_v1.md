## JAIMINI DOCTRINE — ATMAKARAKA

**Jaimini astrology** is a distinct sub-system within Vedic tradition, attributed to the sage Jaimini. Where Parashara Vedic (the primary Jyotish system) uses Vimshottari Dasha and standard planetary rulerships, Jaimini uses a different timing system (Chara Dasha) and a unique set of "karaka" (significator) planets determined by each planet's degree in its sign.

**The Atmakaraka (AK):** "Atman" = soul; "karaka" = significator. The Atmakaraka is the planet at the **highest degree within its sign** (excluding lunar nodes). It is the soul's own significator — the planet that carries the primary karmic agenda, the most burning evolutionary question, and the central direction of the soul's development across lifetimes.

**Computation:** Each planet's sidereal longitude is taken modulo 30° (degree within its sign). The planet with the highest modular degree is the Atmakaraka.
