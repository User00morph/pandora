## DOCTRINE

**Medical Astrology** (Iatromathematics in Greek medicine; Astromedica in Renaissance tradition) is the application of astrological analysis to the body — mapping planetary and sign placements to physiological systems, constitutional tendencies, developmental strengths and vulnerabilities, and health optimization directions.

**Melothesia** (from Greek *melos* — limb, and *thesis* — placement) is the specific doctrine of body-part-to-sign correspondence. Each zodiac sign rules specific body regions; each planet rules specific organs, systems, and physiological functions.

**Core doctrinal sources:**
- Hellenistic: Claudius Ptolemy (*Tetrabiblos*, Book III); Vettius Valens
- Medieval: Guido Bonatti (*Liber Astronomiae*); Al-Qabisi
- Renaissance: Marsilio Ficino (*De Vita*); Paracelsus
- Modern: Eileen Nauman (*Medical Astrology*); Judith Hill

**Interpretive layers:**
1. **Sign melothesia** — what body region each sign governs
2. **Planetary physiology** — what each planet governs in the body
3. **Elemental constitution** — the body's elemental balance and constitutional type
4. **Dominant and afflicted configurations** — which systems are strongest, which need support
5. **Developmental themes** — what the body is here to learn, work with, or master

**Important note:** This overlay provides archetypal/constitutional information for integration into D.B.S's holistic body sovereignty framework. It is not medical diagnosis. All physiological intelligence from this layer is held as constitutional tendency and developmental direction, not as predictive pathology.
