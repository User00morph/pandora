## DOCTRINE

Astrocartography (Jim Lewis, 1976) maps the lines across the Earth's surface where each natal planet would have been on the MC (Midheaven), ASC (Ascendant), IC (Imum Coeli), or DSC (Descendant) at the moment of birth. Living near or visiting a planetary line activates that planet's archetypal function in the lived experience of that location.

**MC line:** The planet on the MC at birth — the zone of maximum public visibility, career identity, and achievement ambition. Living under a planet's MC line puts that planet "over your head" in the vocational domain.

**ASC line:** The planet on the ASC at birth — the zone where that planet shapes your bodily presence, first impression, and environmental encounter. Living on an ASC line embodies that planet in the most personal, immediate way.

**IC line:** The opposite of MC — the zone where the planet would have been at the nadir at birth. IC lines affect the home, roots, private life, and inner foundation.

**DSC line:** The partner/other zone — where that planet shapes relationship encounters and significant others.

The most significant lines for location selection: **Jupiter** (expansion, abundance, learning), **Venus** (beauty, pleasure, relational ease), **Sun** (vitality, visibility, identity). The most challenging but powerful: **Saturn** (discipline, structure, demands), **Pluto** (transformation, intensity), **Mars** (action, conflict, energy).
