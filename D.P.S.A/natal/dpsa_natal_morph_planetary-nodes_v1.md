## DOCTRINE (SUMMARY)

Every planet has heliocentric nodes — the points where its orbital plane crosses the ecliptic. These are distinct from the Moon's nodes (which are Earth-perspective, geocentric nodes of the Moon's orbit around Earth). Planetary nodes represent the **karmic axis of each planet's function** — where that planet crosses into and out of the ecliptic plane, creating the evolutionary forward pull (N.Node) and the inherited pattern (S.Node) for that planet's archetypal principle.

They move extremely slowly — slower than the outer planets — and are effectively generational or trans-generational reference points. Their natal position indicates the **soul-level karmic direction** for each planetary function, independent of the planet's current sign placement.
