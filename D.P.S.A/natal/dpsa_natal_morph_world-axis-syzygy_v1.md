## SECTION A: WORLD AXIS / ARIES POINT

### What is the World Axis
The World Axis consists of the four cardinal ingress points: **0° Aries, 0° Cancer, 0° Libra, 0° Capricorn**. Any natal planet or angle within approximately 2° of these points is "on the World Axis" — directly connected to collective consciousness, public manifestation, and events of transpersonal significance. Planets on the World Axis do not remain purely personal in their expression; their energy projects outward into the collective field.

**Standard orb:** 2° (strict) to 3° (broader)

## SECTION B: PRE-NATAL SYZYGY

### What is the Pre-Natal Syzygy
The pre-natal syzygy is the last New Moon or Full Moon that occurred *before* the moment of birth. In Hellenistic astrology (Paulus Alexandrinus, Vettius Valens), this lunation is a significant sensitive point — it represents the last celestial *reset* before the soul's entry into incarnation. The sign, house, and ruling planet of the syzygy indicate the archetypal quality of the final preparation before birth.

**Distinct from:** Evolutionary astrology's pre-natal eclipse (which looks at the last solar/lunar eclipse, a rarer event). The syzygy is the more granular monthly reset.
