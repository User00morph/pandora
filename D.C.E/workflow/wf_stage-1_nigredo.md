# D.C.E Stage 1 — NIGREDO: The Idea Arrives

## Purpose
Capture the creative impulse exactly as it arrived — raw, unfiltered, complete. No editing. No judgment. The idea is received.

## Inputs
- A thought, observation, resonance, or impulse from any source
- May come from: D.R.D reconstruction, daily experience, other department work, conversation

## Process
1. Capture immediately. Do not let the idea cool.
2. Record in this exact format:
```
[YYYY-MM-DD] [MEDIUM — YouTube / Thread / Article / etc.]:
[The idea in 1-3 sentences exactly as it arrived]
Source/state: [what generated this]
Series: [which series or 'new series']
Connected department: [D.R.D / D.S.E / D.B.S / etc. or 'standalone']
Urgency: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
```
3. File to: `D.C.E/ideas/dce_idea_[topic]_[YYYY-MM-DD].md`
4. Add one-line summary to ref_dce.md active state table under "Idea Queue"

## Done looks like
A filed idea capture. Unpolished. Unedited. The raw signal preserved.

## What NOT to do here
Do not develop the idea. Do not judge its quality. Do not compare it to existing content. Do not decide the format. All of that is Stage 2.

## Output goes to
`D.C.E/ideas/` → Stage 2 (Albedo) when it moves from queue to active development
