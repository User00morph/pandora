## PURPOSE
Test the idea against the sovereign standard. An idea that cannot survive these questions should not receive resources. An idea that passes them has earned its place.

## PROCESS

Run the five purification tests:

1. **ALIGNMENT** — Does this serve the Pandora Empire mission? Does it connect to at least one Triple Helix pillar (STIS / RWIS / PNC-AI) or the long-term sovereign estate vision? If it connects to nothing — why is it here?
2. **SOVEREIGNTY CHECK** — Does this expand or contract sovereignty? Expands: generates income, builds skill, produces assets, increases freedom, serves others genuinely. Contracts: creates dependency, dilutes focus, serves someone else's agenda.
3. **RESOURCE REALITY** — What does this actually cost? Time (hours/week, duration), Money (startup, ongoing), Attention (what gets displaced), Energy (current bandwidth).
4. **UNIQUENESS QUESTION** — What does Morph specifically bring that someone else cannot replicate easily? If nothing — reconsider. If clear — that is the foundation.
5. **TIMING QUESTION** — Why now? The 2027 threshold is the strategic frame — does this serve the pre-threshold build or distract from it?
