Every candlestick encodes the aggregate internal states — fear, greed, certainty, doubt, capitulation, euphoria — of every participant interacting with that instrument at that moment. Most traders either:
- Operate purely from technical indicators without understanding what those indicators actually measure (lagging reflections of collective behavior)
- Operate from emotion and become the pattern instead of observing it
- Lack a unified system that connects market mechanics to the deeper architecture of how reality and consciousness interact

**For whom?**
Morph — as both practitioner and eventual architect of sovereign wealth systems for others.

**What currently exists?**
No trading intelligence system exists within Pandora OS. Starting from zero.

**Why is the current state insufficient?**
Without a structured, continuously updatable system rooted in both technical mastery AND sovereign awareness, market engagement defaults to either institutional mimicry or emotional reactivity. Neither serves the Sovereign.
