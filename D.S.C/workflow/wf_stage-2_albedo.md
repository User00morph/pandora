# WF_DSC — STAGE 2: ALBEDO
**Purification — Test the idea against the sovereign standard.**

---

## WHAT THIS STAGE IS

An idea that cannot survive these tests should not receive resources. An idea that passes has earned its place. The tests are honest or they are useless.

---

## ACTION — The Five Purification Tests

**TEST 1 — ALIGNMENT**
Does this serve the Pandora Empire mission? Does it connect to at least one of the three Triple Helix pillars (STIS / RWIS / PNC-AI) or the long-term sovereign estate vision? If it connects to nothing — why is it here?

**TEST 2 — SOVEREIGNTY CHECK**
Does this expand or contract sovereignty?
- Expands: generates income, builds skill, produces assets, increases freedom, serves others genuinely
- Contracts: creates dependency, dilutes focus, serves someone else's agenda at sovereign cost

Some opportunities look like expansion and are contraction in disguise. Be honest.

**TEST 3 — RESOURCE REALITY** *(non-negotiable)*
What does this actually cost?
- Time: hours per week, for how long?
- Money: startup costs, ongoing costs?
- Attention: what gets displaced if this enters the OS?
- Energy: is current bandwidth available?

**TEST 4 — UNIQUENESS QUESTION**
What does Morph specifically bring to this that someone else cannot replicate easily? If the answer is nothing — reconsider. If the answer is clear — that is the foundation.

**TEST 5 — TIMING QUESTION**
Why now? Is now actually the right time? The 2027 threshold is the current strategic frame — does this serve the pre-threshold build or distract from it?

---

## OUTPUT

An Albedo evaluation document — each test answered honestly, including the uncomfortable answers. A failed test is not automatic rejection — it is a design constraint that must be addressed.

File as: `dsc_project_[name]_albedo.md`

---

## GATE

> Must pass at least 4 of 5 tests.
> Test 3 (Resource Reality) is non-negotiable — must be answered clearly regardless of other outcomes.
> If resource reality is unclear, that becomes Stage 3's first task.

**PASS → load `wf_stage-3_citrinitas.md`**
**FAIL → document why. Either refine the idea or dissolve it. Do not proceed with an unresolved Test 3.**
