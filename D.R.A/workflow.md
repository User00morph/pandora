# WF_DRA_PROPERTY_PIPELINE
**D.R.A — Department of Real Estate & Sacred Architecture**
**Workflow: From Lead to Acquisition or Design**

---

## WHAT THIS WORKFLOW IS

Two pipelines that share this workflow depending on the trigger — the real estate acquisition pipeline and the sacred architecture design pipeline. Both follow the same alchemical stages but diverge in their specific actions.

---

## TRIGGER

**Real Estate Pipeline:**
- A property lead from RWIS wholesale activity
- A market opportunity identified through research
- A specific acquisition target for the sovereign estate vision

**Architecture Pipeline:**
- A space design project (room, building, estate)
- A design research question about materials, proportion, or systems
- A renovation or improvement plan for an existing space

---

## PIPELINE A — REAL ESTATE ACQUISITION

### STAGE 1 — LEAD INTAKE (Nigredo)
```
Document the property opportunity:
- Address / location
- Source of lead (wholesale list, MLS, network, driving)
- Asking price
- Property type and condition (if known)
- Initial appeal — why is this worth looking at?

OUTPUT: dra_lead_[address-shorthand]_raw.md
```

### STAGE 2 — DEAL ANALYSIS (Albedo)
```
SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_deal-analysis.md

Run the full deal analysis process:
- Numbers (acquisition, rehab, carrying, ARV or rental income)
- Comparables
- Risk identification and assessment
- Sovereignty check — does this serve the long-term vision?

OUTPUT: dra_analysis_[address]_eval.md
```

**GATE:** Proceed only if the numbers work AND it serves the strategic vision.

### STAGE 3 — DUE DILIGENCE (Citrinitas)
```
Deep investigation before committing:
- Title search
- Inspection findings
- Zoning and legal review
- Financing structure
- Timeline to close

OUTPUT: dra_dd_[address]_report.md
```

### STAGE 4 — ACQUISITION (Rubedo)
```
Execute the purchase:
- Negotiate final terms
- Close the deal
- File all documents
- Add to portfolio tracker
- Update D.S.E with financial impact

OUTPUT: dra_acquisition_[address]_complete.md
```

---

## PIPELINE B — SACRED ARCHITECTURE DESIGN

### STAGE 1 — VISION CAPTURE (Nigredo)
```
Document the design intention:
- What space is being designed? (room, building, estate element)
- What is the purpose of this space?
- Who will use it and how?
- What feeling or function should it produce?

OUTPUT: dra_vision_[space-name]_raw.md
```

### STAGE 2 — PRINCIPLES RESEARCH (Albedo)
```
SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_source-evaluation.md, skill_environment-audit.md

Research the architectural principles relevant to this design:
- Proportion and geometry (golden ratio, sacred geometry)
- Natural materials appropriate for this application
- Historical precedents — how were similar spaces built?
- Environmental factors (orientation, light, airflow)

OUTPUT: dra_research_[space-name]_principles.md
```

### STAGE 3 — DESIGN (Citrinitas)
```
Produce the design document:
- Layout and spatial relationships
- Material selections with reasoning
- Systems integration (water, air, light, energy)
- Relationship to surrounding environment
- Budget estimate

SKILLS TO LOAD: skill_system-design.md
OUTPUT: dra_design_[space-name]_blueprint.md
```

### STAGE 4 — BUILD / IMPLEMENT (Rubedo)
```
Execute the design:
- Construction planning and sequencing
- Material sourcing
- Build supervision or execution
- Quality verification against the design
- Completion documentation

OUTPUT: dra_build_[space-name]_complete.md
```
