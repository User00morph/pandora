# SKILL — DEAL ANALYSIS
**Loadable by: D.R.A, D.S.E**

---

## WHAT THIS SKILL IS

The ability to evaluate a property deal, business opportunity, or financial decision against a structured set of criteria before committing resources. Loaded when any investment, acquisition, or significant financial decision is on the table.

---

## THE PROCESS

```
1. DEAL SUMMARY
   State the deal in one sentence.
   What is being offered, at what cost, and what is the expected return?

2. SOURCE AND MOTIVATION
   Where did this deal come from?
   Who is offering it and why?
   What is their incentive structure?

3. NUMBERS — RAW
   All financial data laid out without narrative:
   - Acquisition cost
   - Carrying costs (monthly/annual)
   - Repair/renovation estimates
   - Expected revenue or resale value
   - Timeline to return

4. COMPARABLE ANALYSIS
   What do similar deals look like in the current market?
   Is this deal above, below, or at market?
   What data supports that comparison?

5. RISK IDENTIFICATION
   What could go wrong?
   List every risk — financial, legal, timing, condition, market.
   Do not filter for likelihood yet — just name them all.

6. RISK ASSESSMENT
   For each risk identified:
   - How likely is it? (low / medium / high)
   - How severe if it happens? (minor / significant / deal-breaking)
   - Is there a mitigation?

7. SOVEREIGNTY CHECK
   Does this deal expand or contract sovereignty?
   Does it create dependency on any external party?
   Does it serve the long-term vision or just short-term gain?

8. DECISION
   Based on all of the above:
   PROCEED / NEGOTIATE / HOLD / WALK AWAY
   State the reasoning in one paragraph.
```
